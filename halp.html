Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my profile picture?

You can change your profile picture by going to your profile settings and clicking on the profile picture upload option.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset it.

How can I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter inappropriate content, click on the report button next to the post, and provide a reason for the report.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username in the profile settings. Note that usernames must be unique.

How do I manage my privacy settings?

You can manage your privacy settings in the settings section under "Privacy Settings". Here you can adjust who can see your profile and posts.
